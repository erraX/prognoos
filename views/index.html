Prognoos

A knowledge base inference system

By@niminjie

With the development of the Internet, and the information, the propose of ontology, algorithm plays important role to expand the knowledge base and ontology. How to use prediction algorithms to help us extending, completing, improving knowledge base is a research trend.

Ontology can describe knowledge base, entities and relations in ontology can build up a complete network, but data mining in the ontology is inefficient and has low prediction rate. So It is important to prediction relation in ontology. In this research, we put great emphasis on prediction relation in complex network.

We design and implement a relation prediction system. This system could predict relation between two entities and make rule inference. In the backend, template engine we designed can convert rules to SPARQL.
